How It Works

👤

Privacy-First Profiles

We show only initials, age ranges, and regions. No identifying photos or placement details are ever shared.

👍

Community Voting

Votes affect campaign visibility and help direct attention to urgent needs. They never influence placement decisions.

💰

Direct Donations

100% of your donation goes to the campaign, home, or program you choose. Transparent allocation breakdowns for every recipient.

📋

Licensed Partners

We only work with state-licensed foster homes and accredited programs that meet strict child welfare standards.

📊

Impact Tracking

See exactly how your donations and votes make a difference with real-time progress updates on every campaign.

🔒

Secure Platform

Your data and payment information are protected with bank-level encryption. We never charge without your explicit consent.
