# Testimonial Profile Picture Prompts

Prompts for Nano Banana Pro (or similar image generators) to create portrait photos for each testimonial in the "Voices From Our Community" section.

**Consistency guidelines shared across every prompt** — pass these along with each individual prompt so the whole set feels like it belongs on the same site:

- Square 1:1 composition, shoulders-up portrait
- Warm, soft natural lighting (window light or golden hour)
- Shallow depth-of-field, slightly blurred neutral background
- Authentic, documentary-style — not overly polished corporate headshot
- Subject makes gentle eye contact with the camera, small natural smile or calm expression
- No text, no logos, no watermarks
- Photorealistic, high quality, 85mm portrait lens look
- Clothing should feel real and season-appropriate, not branded
